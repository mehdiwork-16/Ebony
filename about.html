ABOUT US

✦ The Beginning

Born from a
Vision of Luxury

EBONY Lounge was conceived in 2021 by founder Aleksander Wiśniewski — a vision to create the definitive premium nightlife experience in Warsaw. A space where African rhythm meets European sophistication.

The name EBONY speaks of our essence: deep, rich, and timeless. Like the precious wood, we stand for quality that endures, beauty that commands attention, and experiences that leave lasting impressions.

From our hand-selected spirits to our bespoke cocktail program crafted by internationally trained mixologists, every element of EBONY is designed to exceed expectation.
